Storytelling Through Video

I'm a video editor with a strong foundation in storytelling; especially when it comes to setting the tone, evoking emotion through music, and crafting compelling intros. My path began on YouTube, where I started a channel called JohnWriting to document my journey of becoming a novelist. But after 18 months I realized I was more passionate about storytelling through video than writing. That realization set me on the path to become a freelance video editor. And now I'm seeking to put my skills to use in service of a mission that matters.

Uncovering a Forgotten Insight of St. Therese

A Culmination of Years of Learning

This was my most ambitious video yet: a full video essay blending narrative, sound design, and motion graphics to explore an often-overlooked perspective of St. Therese. Building on my first deep dive into After Effects, I pushed the visuals even further to create a richer experience.

I handled everything from research to final cut, with generous feedback from friends and family along the way. In the end, this project allowed me to push myself and apply many different aspects of my creative skillset to a single undertaking.
